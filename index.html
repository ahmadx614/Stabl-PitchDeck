The Problem

Strength training is booming globally, but lifters rely on generic sneakers or outdated shoes like Converse, which aren’t designed for lifting biomechanics.

Mainstream brands like Nike, Adidas, and Puma market padded jogging shoes as “gym shoes,” lacking the stability, grip, and support serious athletes need.

There’s a clear gap for purpose-built lifting footwear to go mainstream.

The Solution

Stabl builds purpose-engineered footwear for lifters who don’t compromise.

High-ankle, flat-soled, sturdy, and stylish—like cleats for the gym. Our trainers maximize ground grip, stability, and ankle support for better form and stronger lifts.

Our Product

  • • High-top design for ankle support
  • • Flat, grounded sole for lifting stability
  • • Variants: Heavy-duty canvas and premium leather
  • • Designed aesthetics, practical functionality
  • • Built by lifters, for lifters

Rooted in Ancient Wisdom: Inspired by Hindu and Vedic traditions, emphasizing grounded postures like yoga asanas and deep squats for balance and power.

Performance Driven: Low-profile sole, reinforced heel, grippy outsole—engineered for heavy lifts and dynamic workouts.
